# Neon Orbit

Neon Orbit is a fast-paced, synthwave-inspired top-down arcade survival game built entirely with HTML5 Canvas, CSS3, and vanilla JavaScript. Defend your high-tech reactor core from endless waves of enemies, collect XP to level up your arsenal, and unleash devastating EMPs to clear the screen!

## Features

- **Dynamic Gameplay:** Defend your core with a rotating energy shield that blocks enemy projectiles and physical attacks.
- **Synthwave Aesthetics:** Enjoy a premium visual experience featuring a 3D animated perspective grid, glowing particle trails, chromatic aberration damage effects, and a massive neon sun.
- **RPG Progression:** Collect XP gems from defeated enemies to level up and choose from powerful upgrades (Perks) such as Auto-Turrets, Orbiting Drones, Chain Lightning, and increased movement speed.
- **Combo System:** Chain blocks and kills without taking damage to multiply your score and earn passive healing.
- **Various Enemy Types:** Face off against Weavers, Splitters, Snipers, Gravity Wells, Phantoms (which bypass your shield!), and the massive Dreadnought Boss.

## Controls

- **[W, A, S, D]**: Move your reactor core around the screen.
- **[MOUSE]**: Aim your energy shield to intercept attacks.
- **[SHIFT]**: Dash in the direction you are moving (Costs 20 Energy).
- **[SPACE]**: Unleash an EMP shockwave to destroy all enemies on screen (Requires a full blue Energy Bar).
- **[ESC]**: Pause / Resume the game.

## How to Play

1. **Defend the Core:** Your primary objective is to survive. Move your core and use your shield to block enemy fire. 
2. **Level Up:** Enemies drop yellow XP gems. Collect them to level up and select powerful perks.
3. **Deflect and Destroy:** Snipers fire lasers at you. Use your shield to deflect them back!
4. **Manage Energy:** Destroying enemies fills your blue Energy Bar. Once full, press Space for an EMP.
5. **Beat the Boss:** Every few thousand points, a massive Dreadnought Boss will spawn. Focus fire and stay alive!

## Installation

No installation required! This game runs directly in your web browser.

1. Clone or download the repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari).
3. Click "START SYSTEM" and enjoy!

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5 Canvas:** For rendering high-performance 2D game graphics.
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** For all game logic, physics, entity management, and state machines.
- **CSS3:** For premium glassmorphism UI styling and animated elements.
- **Web Audio API:** For dynamically generated 8-bit / synthwave sound effects.
